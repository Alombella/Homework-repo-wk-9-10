Research in the internet age is easier than ever. With libraries moving most of their resources online it also increases accessibility. You are able to search up anything on the internet and you can find scholarly sources within seconds. You can also find sources that are not reputable as well, you just have to know where to look.

Books for research purposes are becoming more and more obsolete as the age of online text starts to take over. Hard copy book requires you to read through in hopes you find some information related to what you are looking for. Online text lets you search the entirety of the text for key words or phrases, and finding information is much easier.

Mondays class with Joy Hansen was very helpful. She handed out a APA pamphlet that shows you the basics of APA and how to cite anything hardcopy and things you mind find on the internet. This was the most helpful way to learn more about APA formats.
